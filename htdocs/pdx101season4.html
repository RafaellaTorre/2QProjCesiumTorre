PRODUCE X 101

season 4

Description

Produce X 101 is the last and final season of the Produce series' Korean franchise before the series' vote manipulation was publicized. Although it's aim is also to debut an 11-member group like the first two seasons, only 10 of the members will be based on the votes from the season's finale episode, the last member, also known as the X trainee, would be determined by who had the highest accumulated votes throughout the series' run, excluding the top 10. This season also features the additional position X during the program's position evaluation which consists of a combination of two positions, rap and dance or vocal and dance. Produce X 101 was the very first and only season where trainees from the big 3 companies, JYP Entertainment,SM Entertainment and YG Entertainment.

Some notable trainees in this season are:

  • Kim Wooseok and Lee Jinhyuk - who were members of the boygroup UP10TION
  • Han Seungwoo and Choi Byungchan - who were members of the boygroup VICTON
  • Kim Minkyu and Kang Minhee - who got the nicknames "만찢남" or "Manjijnam" because they look like they came out of a webtoon/manga
  • Son Dongpyo - who was the center for "X1-MA"
  • Nam Do hyon - who was called a music genius by the mentors

X1

X1 (stylized: 엑스원)holds the meaning "10 + 1" as "X" is the Roman numeral for 10 and combined with the cardinal number, 1, to represent the group's 11 members. The group debuted on August 27, 2019 with the mini-album "비상 : Quantum Leap", which had the title track, "Flash" featured the group's version of "U Got It" and "MOVE" which were performed on Produce X 101's concept evaluation, and "X1-MA" as a CD-Only track. Although, the group's contract was valid for five years, due to the Produce Rigging Controversy, the group went on a hiatus, starting on November 10, 2019. On January 6, 2020, representatives from each of the members' agencies met up and voted on whether or not X1 would continue their activities, the condition was that the group would only continue if all the companies were in agreement. Dispatch reported that out of the 9 companies, 4 were in agreement, 4 disagreed, and the remaining company's vote was voided due to the uncertainty on how the ballot would be read. As 4 companies disagreed, it was concluded that the group would not continue anymore and it was announced that X1 officially disbanded later that day.

After X1's disbandment, Kim Yohan debuted as a soloist on August 25, 2020 and then redebuted as a member of WEi on October 5, 2020; he also debuted as an actor through the drama, "A Love So Beautiful". Kim Wooseok returned to UP10TION, and focused on his career as a soloist, on May 25, 2020, by releasing his album 1st Desire. (Greed). In a similar matter, Han Seungwoo returned to VICTON, debuted as a soloist on August 10, 2020 and the group also made a comeback on November 4, 2020 with their mini-album "Nostalgia". Song Hyeongjun and Kang Minhee both debuted under the boygroup "CRAVITY" on April 14, 2020, alongside 7 of their labelmates. Cho Seungyoun returned to being a soloist and made a comeback on June 29, 2020, with the mini-album, "Equal". Son Dongpyo debuted under the boygroup Mirae (previously known as DSP Sprouts) on March 17, 2021. Nam Dohyun and Lee Hangyul formed to duo "H&D" ib April 21, 2020 and debuted with the group "BAE173" later that year, on November 19. Cha Junho debuted with "DRIPPIN" (formerly known as W Project 4) on October 28, 2020, by releasing their mini-album, "Boyager". And lastly, Lee Eunsang released his single, "Beautiful Scar", which marked his solo debut on August 31, 2020.
