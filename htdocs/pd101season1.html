PRODUCE 101

season 1

Description

Produce 101, aired on fridays of 2016, from January 22 to April 1, is the first season of Mnet's reality-survival show, gathering 101 trainees from 46 different companies, with the goal of forming a girl group "produced" by the nation. Throughout the series, the trainees are given the chance to showcase their charms, personalities and skills through evaluations, snippets of their dorm life, and individual interviews, giving each of them the chance to appeal themself to the Korean audience, or as referred to in the show, national producers, who hold the votes that determine the fate of their dreams.They all share the same stage and the same dream, but out of the 101 trainees, only the top 11, with the highest number of votes on the last episode, can turn their hopes into reality by debuting in the girl group which was later on known to be I.O.I

Some notable trainees in this season are:

  • Jeon Somi and Kim Sejeong - who were both very popular and often competed for the no.1 spot, with Sejeong snatching the spot for every ranking announcement but Somi eventually winning first in the end.
  • Choi Yoojung - who is first ever center in the whole Produce series, as she was the center for "Pick Me"; which gave a great boost to her popularity, leading her to get third place, earning her a spot in the debut team.
  • Heo Chanmi - who is known as the first subject of Mnet's notorious "evil editing"
  • Jung Chaeyeon - who the widely used term "ending fairy" was coined for, due to her iconic ending pose in her Into the New World performance
  • Kim Sohye - who was known as a "miracle trainee" as she showed drastic improvement in the show despite having no prior training

I.O.I

I.O.I (stylized 아이오아이) is the letter-form of 101 derived from the name of the show Produce 101 and was given the meaning Ideal of Idol. The group debuted on May 5,2016 with their mini-album "Chrysalis", containing the group's version of Crush and When The Cherry Blossoms Fade which the group previously performed in the final episode of Produce 101 for the debut evaluation and Dream Girls which is the album's title track. This EP was then followed by the formation and debut of their 7-member sub-unit on June 10, 2016 with a Korean remake of Salt-N-Pepa's Whatta Man. Their run hit its peak at the release of "Miss Me?" with the title track "Very Very Very" which earned them their first music show win and a perfect all-kill on all korean digital charts. The group continuing on to win best new artist on Golden Disk Awards(GDA), Mnet Asian Music Awards(MAMA), and Seoul Music Awards(SMA). Despite the group's unrelenting success, they released their disbandment single, "Downpour" on January 17, 2017, shortly followed 3-day disbandment concert titled, Timeslip on January 20-22; due to the expiration of their contracts.

The 11 members returned to their respective agencies with Somi leaving JYP to sign with YG Black Label and later on debuted as a solo artist. Sejeong and Mina debuted in Gugudan alongside with Kim Nayoung who also participated in Produce 101, the three of them forming the sub-unit SeMiNa and the two former I.O.I members eventually launching individual careers in music and acting. Choi Yoojung also proceeded to debut in Weki Meki with another of I.O.I's former members, Kim Doyeon and the two made their debut in the acting scene together in the webdrama, Single & Ready to Mingle.Chungha also debuted as a solo artist by releasing her single "Why Don't You Know" wherein some fans found her using choreographies referencing the former I.O.I members as she performed on music shows. Sohye returned to acting after taking a break as she wanted to separate herself from being a singer before starting a career in acting. Jieqiong took up the stage name "Pinky" and debuted with her labelmate and I.O.I's leader, Nayoung under the girlgroup Pristin. And lastly, Yeonjung debuted in WJSN also known as Cosmic Girls, Starship's Korean-Chinese girl group, in collaboration Yuehua Entertainment.
